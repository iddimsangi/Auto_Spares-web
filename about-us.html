About Us

Able leadership and strategic planning have enabled Dhanya Auto Spare Parts to serve our customers with quick and responsive service. Our journey from a small company to a growing leader in this segment, has taught us that speed and quality is of the essence. At Dhanya Auto Spare Parts, we pride ourselves on being one limb of the exciting future of the Dhanya Group and strive towards exceeding our goals by delivering our best. From durable sealed batteries to vehicle lubricants, from axles to filters to break parts and chemicals, we sell them all. These products are sourced from exclusive and authorised dealers of the manufacturers. We use oil from Shell, BP and Castrol while our batteries are sourced from AC Delco, INCOE, Rocket and Energizer. We also sell genuine spare parts for vehicles from Kia and Hyundai. Enjoy the ease with which you can find the parts you are looking for as we have a huge wellorganized inventory. We also supply our sister concerns with automobile lubricants for their car care services. Our dedicated customer service staff will efficiently handle your queries and help you place orders easily.
